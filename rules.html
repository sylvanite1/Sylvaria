Sylvaria Official Rulebook

This is the official rule book for Sylvaria. In this document, you will find the rules intended to keep the server fair and fun for all. If you see anyone breaking these rules, please make a ticket. And also, the admins are not above the rules. If you see a staff member breaking rules, then message the owner.

Admins may punish members before they have broken the rules in cases where they feel it's necessary (threatening to grief, or DDoS, or raid the server). Additionally, the length of the punishment is to be determined based on the severity of the rule that has been broken.

If you have any complaints or questions about the rules, then make a ticket.
