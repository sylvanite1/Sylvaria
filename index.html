About Sylvaria

Create your civilization and write its history

Create Your Own Civilization

Sylvaria is a unique Minecraft server that combines the creative freedom of worldbuilding with the strategic depth of geopolitics. Players can establish their own nations, develop economies, form alliances, and engage in diplomacy or conflict.

Our custom plugins allow for detailed political systems, resource management, and territorial control. Whether you want to build a peaceful trading empire or a military superpower, Sylvaria offers the tools and community to make it happen.

With an active player base and dedicated staff, we've created a living world where your decisions matter and your nation's story unfolds with every action you take.

Server Features

Everything you need to build your empire

Nation Building

Establish your own nation with custom flags, governments, and laws. Claim territory and build your capital city with our advanced claiming system.

Diplomacy System

Form alliances, declare wars, establish trade agreements, and engage in complex international relations with other nations.

Economy & Trade

Custom economy with resource scarcity, trade routes, and global markets. Build wealth through commerce and strategic resource management.

Military & Defense

Raise armies, build fortifications, and develop military strategies to protect your interests and expand your influence.

Lore & History

Document your nation's history, create cultural traditions, and develop a rich backstory for your civilization with our lore system.

Active Community

Join a vibrant community of worldbuilders and geopolitics enthusiasts. Regular events and collaborative projects keep the server dynamic.

Server Rules

Keeping our community fair and enjoyable for everyone

  • Respect All Players

    Treat all players with respect regardless of nationality, race, gender, or beliefs. Harassment and hate speech are strictly prohibited.

  • No Griefing

    Respect other players' builds and territories. Griefing or destroying property without permission is not allowed.

  • No Cheating or Exploits

    Using hacked clients, exploits, or any form of cheating will result in immediate and permanent bans.

  • Follow Staff Directions

    Respect and follow instructions from server staff. They are here to ensure everyone has an enjoyable experience.
